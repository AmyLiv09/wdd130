Our Top Adventures

Family Float: Perfect for first-timers and younger adventurers, the Family Float is a gentle journey down calm waters with stunning views. Enjoy splashing through mild ripples, spotting river wildlife, and laughing your way through safe, scenic stretches of the river. Great for families with kids or anyone looking for an easygoing introduction to rafting. No experience necessary—just bring your sense of fun!

Scenic Explorer: A favorite for nature lovers and photographers, the Scenic Explorer trip winds through the lush canyons and tranquil bends of the Wildflower River. You’ll float past waterfalls, red rock walls, and tall evergreens, all while enjoying a relaxing paddle and expert storytelling from your guide. This trip blends calm rapids with wide open views—ideal for couples, friends, or anyone craving a peaceful escape.

Raging Rapids: For thrill-seekers only! The Raging Rapids route tackles Class IV and V whitewater in a canyon full of steep drops, quick turns, and roaring waves. Expect your heart to race and your paddle to stay busy as you conquer the most intense stretch of water we offer. This six-hour trip includes a lunch break by the river and an optional cliff jump for the truly brave. Experience the rush, the roar, and the ride of your life!
